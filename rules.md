# Rules
<small>Applies only to contributing</small>

1. **Do not remove ANY commits without asking first.**
2. **Joke commits are allowed!** Have fun with it.
3. **Do not use AI-generated images or music.** Keep it human-made.
4. **Using AI to generate code or dialogue is allowed**, but keep it clean and functional. We need working code, not slop.
5. **NSFW content is not allowed.** This includes hardcore pornography, explicit sex, and real-life gore. *(Exception: In-game gore that fits the Mogeko Castle aesthetic is fine. Softcore/implied content is TBD.)*
6. **Swearing is allowed**, censored or uncensored.
7. **No bigotry.** Racist, homophobic, sexist, transphobic, or otherwise discriminatory content will not be tolerated. *(The project lead is a transfem lesbian—please be respectful.)*
8. **Softcore/implied sexual content** *(non-explicit)*: TBD—still deciding on this one.
9. **No malicious code.** Do not insert malware, viruses, ransomware, keyloggers, spyware, or any other harmful code into the project.
10. **No self-promotion or spam.** Don't advertise other projects or websites here.
11. **Keep the project rated 16+ to 17+** depending on content and regional standards.

---

**Violation of these rules will result in being blocked from contributing. This decision is final and cannot be appealed.**
